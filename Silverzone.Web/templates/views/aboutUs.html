About Silverzone Foundation

Silverzone Foundation is an NGO, which aims in promoting competitive awareness of academics in school children in India and abroad. Over the years we have been in the process of creating this awareness among the students and school authorities by organizing and conducting International Olympiads. Our Olympiads create a platform for students from all ways of life to appear in various levels of competitive exams in the National/International level. We identify the outstanding performers on the basis of merit and give recognition in the form of Certificates, Medals, Trophies, and Prizes.
