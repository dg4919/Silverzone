NASA, United States of America

Top 2 students of the 3rd level participants will be sent for educational excursion to NASA.

Kennedy Space Center (KSC), NASA, offers a wonderful educational and entertainment experience to students of all ages. Here students get a chance to explore, experience and interact through space shuttle mission simulations. It’s an enriching experience that comes once in a lifetime.

The main aim and objective of the trip is to know NASA’s history, to get first-hand experience of shuttle launch and to know the latest development in the field of space science. The students also get an opportunity to have lunch and interact and photo session with astronauts also to gain first hand information on their memorable experiences in space.

Apart from education two days programme at Kennedy Space Center, NASA students also enjoy the magical attractions of Walt Disney World Resort, Universal Studios along with Disney World Universal Studio.
