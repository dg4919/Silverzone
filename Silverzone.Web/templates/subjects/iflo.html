About International Olympiad of French Language

International French Language Olympiad. French(IFLO) is the second most widely taught language in the world after English. Apart from English, French is spoken in all the five continents. French is also the second language in the world by the number of countries that have adopted it as an official or administrative language. France, fifth commercial power attracts entrepreneurs in various sectors of activities (distribution, automobile, luxury, aeronautics ...), researchers and the best foreign students.

Therefore, learning a foreign language and more specifically Frenchenhancesone's resume helps to build and develop one's career in the long run, and also enriches one's personality, because learning a new language helps open one's mind to a world of new cultures.

The objective of iFLO is therefore, to make French learning an interesting and interactive process in which a learner is actually able to put his/her skills, memory, talent and knowledge to test.

It is good for every child to appear for talent exams because it improves reading & writing skills as well as the overall knowledge of the subject.

You can truly appreciate the importance of knowing a foreign language when you begin to Study it. The feeling of accomplishment you get on learning a foreign language, the personal enrichment that the language brings, can be felt only by experience. Languages are a means of expression. They are the links that bridge diverse cultures. Knowing many languages improves one's cognitive skills and interpretive abilities. Learning a foreign language can indeed be a very rewarding experience.

There are many benefits associated with learning a foreign language at an early age:

Learning a language of another country helps one understand its culture.

Having studied a foreign language, one can understand one's own language better.

Certain courses & colleges require at least two years of studying a foreign language for admission.

Many fields such as tourism, communications, advertising, engineering and education have sectors that require the knowledge of foreign language.

After research & study we have found French to be one of the most important foreign languages and we are hereby pleased to launch this talent exam for the benefit of school students.

About Test Paper

The language medium is French only. The examination is being conducted for classes from 6th to 10th with the following details.

There will be 50 questions and the duration will be of 60 Minutes.

The details of the manner in which the test is to be conducted will be sent with the question papers. The questions will be of objective type in nature with multiple choice answers. There is no negative marking.
