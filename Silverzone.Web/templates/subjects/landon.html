LONDON, United Kingdom

Top 2 students of the 3rd level participants will be sent for a Trip to United Kingdom.

The purpose of this trip is tourism and to explore the country and its rich culture. Here students get a chance to explore the city of London with major attractions like Tower of London, London Eye, Madame Tussauds, Ripley’s Believe It Or Not! - Robert Ripley, the intrepid cartoonist.
