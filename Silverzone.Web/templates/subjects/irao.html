About Reasoning & Mental Ability Olympiad

The International Reasoning & Mental Ability Olympiad, a test of competence and proficiency in History, Geography and Civics is held annually at the National and International levels based on syllabus prescribed by CBSE/ICSE and the State Boards. Detailed syllabus with Sample question paper is provided along with this Information Booklet. Please refer the Books published by Silverzone Foundation for the preparation of IRAO. SilverZone foundation recommends the competition for each and every student in the school.

Fee Structure and Study Material(For Class 3rd to 10th)

Fee Structure and Study Material (for class 6th to 12th) : The participation fee for the Olympiad is Rs.150/- per student. The Study material for schools are available at the discounted price. The price of Reasoning Excellene Guide(optional) is Rs. 200/- for school only. The printed price of the book is Rs. 280/-. The book contains about 80% of the syllabus of the Olympiad.

Fee Structure and Study Material (for class 6th to 12th) : The participation fee for the Olympiad is USD$8 per student. The Study material for schools are available at the discounted price. The price of Reasoning Excellene Guide(optional) is USD$10/- for school only. The printed price of the book is USD$12/-. The book contains about 80% of the syllabus of the Olympiad.

Payment Mode

About Test Paper

The examination is being conducted for all classes from 6th to 12th with the following details.

For Classes 6-12: There will be 50 questions and the duration will be between 60 Minutes.

The details of the manner in which the test is to be conducted will be sent with the question papers. The questions will be of objective type in nature with multiple choice answers. There is no negative marking. For all classes OMR answer sheets will be provided. The students are allowed to retain their question papers, whereas answer sheets shall be returned to the foundation for assessment and marking.
