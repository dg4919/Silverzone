About International Talent Hunt Olympiad

The International Talent Hunt Olympiad, a test of competence and proficiency in Science, Maths and Mental ability, is held annually at the National and International levels, based on syllabus prescribed by CBSE/ ICSE and the State Boards. Apart from syllabus, a part of test paper is based on the application of knowledge. The syllabus for the iTHO' is provided on the last page of this 'Information Brochure'. Please refer the books published by 3GenX for the preparation of iTHO. SSE recommends the competition for each and every student who is studying science and Maths as their subjects of study in the school.

Fee Structure and Study Material

Fee Structure: For Schools in India (for class 1st to 10th) The participation fee for the Olympiad is Rs.100/- per student. The Study material for schools are available at the discounted price. The price of Comprehensive Book is Rs.120/-, and Previous Year Question Paper Booklet is Rs. 150/-which is not included in the fee.

Fee Structure: For Schools Outside India (for class 1st to 10th) The participation fee for the Olympiad is USD $8 per student. The Study material for schools are available at the discounted price. The price of Comprehensive Book is USD$4 and Previous Year Question Paper is USD$8/-, which is not included in the fee.

About Test Paper

The examination is being conducted for all classes from 1st to 10th with the following details.

For Classes 1-2:- The paper will consist of 40 questions and the duration of the exam will be of 60 Minutes.

For Classes 3-10: The paper will consist of 50 questions and the duration of the exam will be of 40 to 60 Minutes.

Important Notes

As iTHO 2017 is being organized at National and International level, due to various reasons, it is not possible to conduct the olympiad on a specific single date. The International Talent Hunt Olympiad will be conducted on 15th December 2017 and 23rd January 2018. Please decide the date which is suitable to your school.
